General Questions

Talent Matrix is a dynamic online platform that serves as a hub for connecting talented individuals with job opportunities across various creative and technical fields. Through Talent Matrix, users can showcase their skills, portfolios, and expertise, while employers can easily discover and hire the perfect talent for their projects. With its intuitive interface and comprehensive features, Talent Matrix empowers both talents and employers to collaborate, innovate, and thrive in the digital landscape.

Talent Matrix aims to provide opportunities for talented people to showcase their skills and earn through their passion. whether you want to earn more by doing side projects apart from your job or you want a free life style talent matrix offers it all. We also make it easy for employers or people who want to get a work done to look for the best people for their job.

Getting Started on Talent Matrix is as simple as it can get. If you are a client looking to get a work done just register as a client on the platform and then search through our pool of talented professionals to get the best person for the job or you can post a job and select the talents for your job. While if you are a skilled individual looking for opportunities then register as a talent on the platform and apply to different job posts by clients.

Good news is we don't charge you until you hire on Talent Matrix. Apart from the certain amount deduction from the project payment we don't charge you.

Talent Questions

For talents we have a talent matrix has a Discord channel where talents can discuss and work on projects if they have any issues with the projects. For any issues on the platform our team is always there to answer you.

Talent matrix has a wide range of skill including photography, videography, web design and so on. You can showcase any kind of skill througn which you would like to help people on talent matrix.

Once you fill the application form we will contact you via e-mail and arrange interviews. This process is to check if the applicant actually possess the skills and is ready to take on client projects.

We actually don't need you to have any educational certification to join talent matrix but we will check your fluency in English and Dzongkha.

Once you are verified clients will either send you hire request or you can apply to the jobs posted by clients.

When you take on a project from a client the client will transfer the amount to talent matrix and on completion of the project the money shall be transfered to the talent.

At talent matrix we allow you to earn through your passions while you live the life you desire. The talent matrix team is always there to answer to your queries and you can also collaborate with other talents on our discord channel.
