About Us

Talent Matrix was established by a group of computer science students at Gyelpozhing College of Information and Technology. Their vision was to create a platform enabling talented individuals nationwide to exhibit their skills and monetize their passions, facilitating a lifestyle aligned with their aspirations. Additionally, the platform serves as a convenient resource for the public to connect with skilled professionals for various projects and endeavors.
